Junior WAEC Mathematics Question

The table below show the performance of students in a French assignment. Use it to answer the following questions.

Marks (x) Number of students (f)
1 2
2 1
3 4
4 6
5 3
6 2
7 5
8 2

(a) How many students took the test?
(b) What is the mode?
(c)Calculate the mean mark.

Solution

(a)How many students took the test simply mean, add all the frequencies together. that is, 2+1+4+6+3+2+5+2 = 25 students.
(b)Mode = mark that appeared most; mark with the highest frequency = 4
(c) Mean(frequency)= summation of f.x/summation of the f

next, we prepare a table showing the items in the formula i.e x, f, fx

marks (x) Frequency (f) fx
1 2 3
2 1 2
3 4 12
4 6 24
5 3 15
6 2 12
7 5 35
8 2 16
Total frequency = 25 Total fx = 118

Mean (frequency) = 118/25 4.72
